Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about dental care and our services.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for regular check-ups and cleanings. However, some patients with specific conditions may need more frequent visits.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. We can help you understand your benefits and submit claims. We also offer financing options for uninsured patients.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and offer flexible payment plans through CareCredit.

Do you offer sedation dentistry?

Yes, we offer several sedation options including nitrous oxide to help patients with dental anxiety feel comfortable during their procedures.

How long does a dental cleaning take?

A typical cleaning appointment takes about one hour. This includes the examination, cleaning, and any necessary x-rays.

What should I expect on my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive examination, digital x-rays, and we'll develop a personalized treatment plan based on your needs and goals.

Can I schedule an emergency appointment?

Yes, we understand dental emergencies happen. Call us immediately at (405) 843-9731 and we'll do our best to fit you in as soon as possible.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. They are a durable and long-term solution for missing teeth.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Professional teeth whitening performed by our dental team is safe and effective. We use high-quality products that are safe for tooth enamel.

What can I do about bad breath?

Bad breath can be caused by various factors. We can help identify the cause and recommend treatment options, from improved hygiene to professional cleaning.
