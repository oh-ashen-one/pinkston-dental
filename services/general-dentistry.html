General Dentistry

Preventive care and routine dental services to keep your smile healthy.

General dentistry is the foundation of maintaining a healthy smile. At Pinkston Dental Care, we provide comprehensive preventive and restorative services designed to keep your teeth and gums in optimal condition.

Our General Services Include

  • + Dental Exams: Comprehensive evaluations to detect issues early
  • + Professional Cleanings: Removal of tartar and plaque buildup
  • + Fillings: Treatment of cavities with tooth-colored materials
  • + Digital X-rays: Advanced imaging for accurate diagnosis
  • + Root Canals: Treatment of infected tooth pulp
  • + Gum Disease Treatment: Professional care for periodontal health

Why Regular Dental Care Matters

Regular dental visits help prevent serious dental problems before they start. Professional cleanings remove buildup that brushing alone cannot eliminate, and routine exams catch problems early when they're easier and less expensive to treat.
