Emergency Dentistry

Fast, compassionate care when dental emergencies strike.

Dental emergencies don't wait for normal business hours. At Pinkston Dental Care, we understand that you need immediate care when faced with severe tooth pain, trauma, or other urgent dental issues.

Emergency Services We Provide

  • + Severe Toothache Treatment: Rapid pain relief and diagnosis
  • + Broken or Chipped Teeth: Immediate restoration services
  • + Knocked-Out Teeth: Emergency care to save your tooth
  • + Dental Abscess: Infection treatment and relief
  • + Lost Fillings or Crowns: Quick replacement
  • + Gum Injury: Professional wound care

What to Do in a Dental Emergency

Call us immediately at (405) 843-9731. Dr. Kirk and our team will work to fit you in as quickly as possible. If it's after hours, follow any emergency instructions on our voicemail.

For severe trauma: If you've experienced significant facial trauma, visit an emergency room first, then contact us for follow-up care.
