About Pinkston Dental Care

Excellence in dental care has been our mission since day one.

Our Story

Pinkston Dental Care was founded with a singular vision: to provide exceptional dental care in a comfortable, professional environment. With over 25 years of experience, Dr. Michael Kirk has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve their healthiest, most beautiful smiles.

Dr. Michael Kirk's Philosophy

Dr. Kirk believes that quality dental care isn't just about treating teeth—it's about understanding each patient's unique needs and goals. Every treatment plan is customized to deliver results that look natural and last a lifetime.
